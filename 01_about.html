About

My name is Andrea Jensen and I am the artist behind the icing bag :)

I was first introduced to cookie decorating when visiting my sister-in-law Amanda one Christmas and she taught me how to use royal icing. Fast forward a year when a friend was fundraising for Cystic Fibrosis (please read more about this horrible disease here) and I offered to make decorated cookies to help out. We raised over $1000 through cookies alone, and I started getting inquiries for custom cookies for baby showers, birthday parties and more. From that humble beginning we are excited to see what the next year will bring!

Why Cookies?

When you give a custom cookie, you give so much more than a delicious, fresh treat - you are telling the person that you thought of them specifically and what they would enjoy. In a world where $5 coffees are commonplace, a custom cookie has a 'wow' factor that a gift card cant beat!

Made with high quality ingredients (local when possible) it is the perfect gift for the person that has everything, a hostess gift, celebrating a new home, baby showers, weddings, birthday favours or party platters, the list can go on!

As you are purchasing from a local Marketer, you can also feel good knowing that you are supporting people in your community too!
